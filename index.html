01 · Profile

A clinical view of evidence, shaped for action.

Kushal Naik is a physician-epidemiologist with more than 10 years of experience across real-world evidence, pharmacoepidemiology, healthcare analytics, clinical research, and AI-enabled data products.

At ZS Associates, he leads cross-functional delivery, product strategy, and go-to-market for RWD- and AI-enabled healthcare analytics across biopharma and a national health plan. His work includes care-gap and medication-adherence analytics, patient journeys, HCP/KOL and expert-network intelligence, scientific-insight synthesis, drug safety, and clinically grounded predictive applications.

Previously, he led FDA- and CMS-facing pharmacoepidemiology and safety-surveillance studies at The SPHERE Institute using national Medicare-linked data. Earlier work spans pediatric registries, transplantation research, oncology, neurology, and cardiovascular outcomes.
